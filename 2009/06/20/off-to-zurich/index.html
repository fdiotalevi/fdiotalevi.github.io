Off to Zurich

Published by Filippo Diotalevi on 20th June 2009 (last update 20th June 2009)

Just preparing my stuff, since tomorrow I'll flight in the early morning to Zurich.

The Swiss city in fact will hold the OSGi Devcon Europe conference, that runs in parallel with Jazoon. After attending many big conferences like Devoxx, Jazoon, Apachecon, I'm really looking forward this small conference. So many things are happening in the OSGi world in these days, and such a small-focused event is ideal to meet all the developers involved. Some of the topics:
  • Distributed OSGi
  • Persistence with OSGi
  • Blueprint service (standardization of dependecy injection a-la Spring DM)
  • Provisioning with Eclipse P2
  • Provisioning with (the upcoming) Apache Ace
  • Scala & OSGi

Of course I'll take the opportunity to walk around the city and take some pictures. Last time here, two years ago, I found an extremely hot weather (but I didn't bring my camera with me). Hopefully I'll have some picture to post next week.
